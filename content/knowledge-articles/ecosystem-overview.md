The atlas is organized around product territories rather than protocol modules. Each region answers a simple question: where does confidential execution create a clear advantage for builders or users? That framing keeps the map legible even when the underlying systems are technically diverse.

## What to Explore Next

If you want the fastest route through the material, start with DeFi, AI, and Payments. Those territories make the value of confidentiality easiest to see. From there, the prediction and consumer regions broaden the story by showing how Arcium can protect strategy, behavior, and user context outside the usual infrastructure narrative.
